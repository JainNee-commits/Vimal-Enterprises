Vimal Enterprises —
Rewa's Original

One of the first building material shops in Rewa. 36 years strong.

Established in 1990, Vimal Enterprises holds the proud distinction of being one of Rewa's very first building material dealerships. Over three and a half decades, we have served thousands of homebuilders, contractors, civil engineers & real estate developers across Rewa and Madhya Pradesh.

Today we are authorised dealers of six of India's most respected brands — Goel TMT, Tata Tiscon, AL Kamdhenu Gold, MP Birla Cement, Tata Wiron & Asian Paints. Our legacy is built on honesty, quality and a deep commitment to Rewa's growth.
